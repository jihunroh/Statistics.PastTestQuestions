<div>
#### 확률변수 설정 ####

$$ A_1, A_2, \cdots, A_8 \overset{iid}{\sim} N(\mu_A , \sigma_ A^2) $$

$$ B_1, B_2, \cdots B_8 \overset{iid}{\sim} N(\mu_B , \sigma_ B^2) $$

$ D_i := A_i - B_i $ 로 정의하면

$$ D_{1}, D_{2}, \cdots, D_{8} \overset{iid}{\sim} N(\delta, \sigma_ D^2) $$

#### 가설 설정 ####

$$ H_0 : \delta = 0 $$

$$ H_1 : \delta \ne 0 $$

</div>

$$ T = \frac{ \bar D }{ S_D / \sqrt n } = -2.68 $$
