#### 추정량 $ U $ 의 확률분포 ####

$$ \begin{eqnarray}
P[U=\frac{1}{3}] &=& 1/2 \\
P[U=\frac{2}{3}] &=& 1/2
\end{eqnarray} $$

#### $ U $ 의 평균제곱오차 ####

$$ \begin{eqnarray}
MSE[U] &=& E\left[ (U - \theta)^2 \right] = V[U] + Bias^2[U] \\
&=& (E[U^2]-E[U]^2) + (E[U] - \theta)^2 \\
&=& (\frac{5}{18} - \left( \frac{1}{2} \right) ^2) + (\frac{1}{2} - \theta)^2 \\
&=& \theta ^2 - \theta + \frac{5}{18}
\end{eqnarray} $$
